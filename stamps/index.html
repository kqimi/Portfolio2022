Postage Stamp Designs

I collaborated with my small group of design cohorts to develop a cohesive set of stamps. We decided to choose four states in the US and design stamps based on their national flower. In the process of creating these four stamps, we contributed our ideas to the thought process, including deciding on using vector art, background, and fonts. Ultimately, we worked together and created this four beautiful stamps.
